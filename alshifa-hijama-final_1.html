Post-Treatment

Hijama Aftercare Guide

After your session we provide dates and water and ask you to rest for 5–10 minutes. A full guide is provided at the end of every session.

🧴

Immediate Hygiene Care

  • No shower for at least 12 hours
  • Apply black seed oil or olive oil to cuts
  • Avoid commercial ointments unless directed
  • Do not scratch treated areas
  • Cup marks fade within 3–15 days
🏃

Activity & Rest

  • Stay awake for at least 2 hours post-treatment
  • Avoid strenuous workouts for 24 hours
  • Light walking is fine and encouraged
  • Avoid physical intimacy for 24 hours
🥗

Dietary Recommendations (48 hrs)

  • Avoid dairy: milk, cheese, yogurt
  • Avoid red meat, fish, processed foods
  • Drink plenty of water and herbal teas
  • Focus on fresh fruits and vegetables
